Working Group Prototype

Morris Township Planning Map

A working map for reviewing walkability and bikeability concerns in Morris Township, with current hotspot records organized for discussion of sidewalk gaps, crossings, and reference destinations.

This prototype presents TAC source material as a clearer civic reference: one map for orientation, one working rail for filtering records, and one place to review what each location represents.

Project Brief

Status
Working-group prototype for review and discussion.
Source Data
TAC hotspot points with contextual township overlays and a curated set of Morris Township reference destinations.
Audience
Morris Township staff, TAC members, and close planning collaborators.

Future Public Input

Resident reports would be reviewed before entering the official planning record.

The current map reflects official working-group data.

Future resident submissions would remain under review until the planning group confirms how they should be used.

Repeated reports and requested destinations would help shape workshops, survey analysis, and stakeholder review.
