Morris Township Walking and Biking Survey

This survey page is designed for the April input phase. It helps residents describe specific trouble spots and destination connections in a format the planning group can review and map more clearly.

Survey responses collected here remain under review. They do not appear as official planning records until the working group has reviewed them.

What to Report

Specific locations and useful travel needs are more valuable than broad complaints.

Two Input Paths

  • Trouble spots: report a sidewalk gap, crossing problem, visibility issue, speeding concern, or accessibility barrier at a specific location.
  • Destination connections: describe where you want to get to, what area you are starting from, and what gets in the way.

How to Answer Well

  • Unsafe crossing at Columbia Turnpike near Woodland School
  • Need safer walking route from neighborhood to Kings
  • Missing sidewalk segment on this block
